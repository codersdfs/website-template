Our Story

A Journey of Flavor

Crafted with Passion & Precision

Sakura was born from a deep reverence for Japanese culinary traditions. Our head chef, trained in the kitchens of Tokyo's prestigious Ginza district, brings over 25 years of expertise to every plate.

We source our ingredients from the finest fishermen in Tsukiji market, local organic farms, and import specialty items directly from Japan — ensuring an authentic experience that transports you to the heart of Tokyo.
